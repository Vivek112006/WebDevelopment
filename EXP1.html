About Our College

DY Patil College of Engineering is one of the leading institutes providing quality education in engineering and technology. The college focuses on innovation, practical knowledge, and overall development of students. It offers modern labs, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented learning.

Our mission is to create skilled engineers who can contribute to society and technology. We provide opportunities in research, projects, internships, and placements.
